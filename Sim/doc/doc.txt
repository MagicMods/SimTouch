- Origin (0,0) at top-left
- (1,1) at bottom-right
- Center at (0.5, 0.5)
- Circular boundary radius: 0.475

## WebGL Transformation
- Single transformation in vertex shader
- Converts [0,1] to [-1,1] clip space
- No other coordinate transformations in the system
